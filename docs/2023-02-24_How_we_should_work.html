How we should work

2023-02-24

My thoughts after reading Powerful: Building a culture of freedom and responsibility. While I dont agree with everything written I did take away the following that I'd like to see at every place I work.

⇒ Foster freedom and resposibility

Open, clear and constant communication about the work to be done and challenges. This should go both ways, from management to individuals and vice versa.

⇒ Every single team member understands the business

Constant communication from management about challenges / oppurtunities.

⇒ Cultivate strong opinions that are weakly held

Argue on facts, change opinions when faced with data.

⇒ Focus on the future

Consistent northstar aligned with the business
